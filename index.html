          Organizational Strategies for Healthcare Leaders

          Evidence-based approaches to improve provider wellness at the systems level

          Reducing Administrative Burden

          • Implement team documentation models

            Distribute documentation tasks among care team members to reduce individual burden

          • Use AI-powered ambient scribes

            Leverage technology to automate clinical documentation and reduce EHR time

          • Streamline prior authorization

            Simplify approval processes and reduce administrative obstacles to patient care

          • Deploy intelligent medication refill protocols

            Automate routine medication renewals within established guidelines

          Optimizing Work Environment

          • Ensure safe staffing ratios

            Maintain adequate provider-to-patient ratios to prevent overwork and burnout

          • Promote schedule flexibility

            Allow providers input in scheduling and support work-life balance

          • Institute real PTO policies

            Ensure time off is genuinely protected and free from work intrusion

          • Create psychological safety culture

            Foster an environment where providers feel safe to speak up and seek help

          Leadership & Culture Change

          • Appoint Chief Wellness Officer

            Designate dedicated leadership role responsible for provider well-being initiatives

          • Normalize mental health conversations

            Reduce stigma by openly discussing mental health and making resources accessible

          • Establish peer support programs

            Create structured programs for providers to support one another

          • Lead by example

            Leadership must model healthy work-life balance and self-care practices
